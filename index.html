About Bercam Group

About

Leaders in ensuring quality procurement.

Bercam Group was born due to the growing need of the international market for a reliable and efficient supplier with products of the highest quality. After 35 years in the honey processing, marketing and export business with more than 8,000 assorted containers and more than 160 satisfied customers around the world, we decided to expand our product portfolio. We believe that our country and our area have delicacies that the world needs to know, we mainly focus on the supply of our clients, ensuring trust, transparency and speed.

1980-1985

Our organization got its start when our founder Federico Berrón realized that we could better serve the increasing national honey market by gathering, selecting and providing the best quality honey from the Yucatan Peninsula.

1985-1989

Due to the increasing international demand for honey, we start exporting our products to the German market gaining our very first global experience.

1989-2003

Having successfully worked with Germany, the second generation started doing business with clients in other European countries, Saudi Arabia, USA, and Colombia.

2003-2019

Because of the high volume we handled, we relocated to Merida, Yucatan and built the first ever certified honey plant in Mexico.

2019-Now

After leading the Mexican honey industry for almost 40 years, the third generation diversifies and increases the product portfolio with the addition of another Mexican delicacy: the octopus maya

About

Mexico is the third largest producer of octopus worldwide?

75% of the national octopus catch occurs in Yucatan.

Octopus Maya has the designation of origin in the state of Yucatan.
Always with traditional fishing gear, totally sustainable, the Octopus Maya produced in this area is of high quality, which together with the process of selection and immediate freezing, translates into an excellent raw material.
Highly appreciated in the Mediterranean countries and North America.
It is considered a delicacy and depending on the culture, it is cooked differently.
It can be eaten raw (carpaccio) when fresh, grilled on an open flame, served in pasta or ceviche, and more.
Mayan Octopus carries a mild and slightly sweet flavor, and a tender chew.

Sizes

1200-1500gr, 800-1200gr, 500-800gr, 300-500gr.
